What is Let's Move US?

Let's Move US is building the first national discovery platform dedicated entirely to adaptive sports and recreation.

Our Mission

To connect individuals with disabilities to adaptive sports and recreation opportunities that improve physical and emotional health, expand employment pathways, and strengthen communities.
